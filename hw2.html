Reflection

As I implemented my design, I used the map() function to dynamically adjust the feature sizes of my bunny in response to mouse movement, creating a more engaging and interactive experience. To further enhance the aesthetic appeal, I added outlines and gradients, which I discovered can significantly enhance visual interest with even simple changes. Through this process, I gained valuable insights into the importance of balancing feature sizes to maintain the bunny's appeal across different scales, as well as the need to understand how different features interact and affect the overall look in order to create coherent variations.
