Assignment 3: Experimental Clock

Reflection

My experimental clock visualizes sleep stages: REM, Light Sleep, and Deep Sleep through different visuals. REM is depicted with colorful swirling ellipses, Light Sleep with gently rotating spirals in calming blues, and Deep Sleep with pulsating green circles. These animations provide a more relaxed perception of time, integrating a subtle clock face within the visuals.

The design process involved three sketches: Colorful Waves for fluid transitions, Geometric Shapes for precision, and the chosen Sleep Stages Visualization for its blend of function and aesthetics. This concept connects time to human experience, unlike the abstract waves or rigid geometric shapes.

This project taught me that clocks can express our relationship with time, balancing functionality with artistic expression. It sparked interest in exploring biological rhythms, showing the power of merging art and technology to enrich our understanding.
