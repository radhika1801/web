Reflection

As I worked on this project, I aimed to create a dynamic optical illusion that would captivate and calm the viewer, using animated blue circles on a gradient background to achieve a sense of simplicity and modernity. Through my research and design process, I delved into the world of optical illusions, studying how motion and color can create mesmerizing effects, and iteratively fine-tuned my design to strike the perfect balance.

The illusion has potential applications as a browser extension, where it could be adapted as a dynamic screensaver or background, or as wearable art, inspiring patterns for wearable technology such as smart clothing or accessories. Overall, the project aims to enhance everyday experiences, providing both aesthetic pleasure and moments of mindfulness.
