Proposal

Carnival Quest is a multi-stage arcade game featuring three engaging mini-games: Ball Catching, Balloon Pop, and Duck Pond. Players begin by selecting from three unique avatars and then progress through each game, honing their reflexes and precision. In the Ball Catching game, players use arrow keys to catch falling balls with a net, with increasing speed adding complexity. The Balloon Pop game challenges players to pop balloons with a dart using quick reflexes before time runs out. Finally, the Duck Pond game requires strategic timing to catch ducks with a net, revealing hidden prizes. With vibrant carnival-themed visuals and captivating sound effects, Carnival Quest offers a fun and challenging experience, with the opportunity to replay and improve scores.
